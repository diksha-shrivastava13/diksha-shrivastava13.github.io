### KaggleX Fellowship

`Fellow | Dec '22 - Mar '23`

I was selected as one of top 152 mentees across 15+ countries and 20+ timezones among more experienced professionals.
I distinctly remember thinking there was no way I will get in, considering the crazy, cracked people in the world. I did.
Since I have been very interested in the field of AI + Neuroscience, with a handful of projects and research in the area,
I had the fortune to get Ms. Usha Rengaraju as my mentor for this program. And yet again, ML people are really, really sweet.

This program opened me to a lot of opportunities and beautiful people, I connected with so many Kaggle Grandmasters and 
participated in their workshops. I also received a grant from Google and sponsored access to Google ML certificate courses.
The deliverable here was developing a project for the final demo day of the Cohort.

I was part of the Cohort-2, and back then we had to choose our own problem space and propose a project. I chose to work on
`Symbolic Music Understanding and Generation`, continuing my work on the problem of generating music that can help the 
Neurodivergent people or anyone struggling with disorders. 

I focused more on the `MIDI music preprocessing, classification and generation parts, music transformers and some research on
symbolic music understanding`. It is due to this experience that I afterwards got into open-source and eventually joined 
the Digital Product School. I'm super grateful for the early mentoring I've received and all the people I met here whom I
look up to.
