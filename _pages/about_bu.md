### Bennett - NVIDIA Centre for Artificial Intelligence

`Student Researcher | Apr '22 - Jul '22`

I worked on `Analysis of Neural Correlates of Different Music Genres using Machine Learning` as first author, 
under mentorship by Dr. Anuj Bharti. This paper was selected for Fechner Day ’22 Conference at Sweden by the `International
Society for Psychophysics`, however I had to withdraw submission since I could not go and present it.

I was in my first year of Bachelors at this time, and it was absolutely crazy to me exploring how the brain interprets 
things for the first time. As an undergraduate, it is really hard to build the trust to obtain medical data in India. 
And I wanted to conduct a study on how music is interpreted by individuals with Bipolar Disorders as I could not find 
a ML, fMRI study on it. 

This experience also coincided with my end-semester exams, and I remember rushing back and forth to the lab in what 20 minutes
I got in between classes. I distinctly recall having to redo fMRI preprocessing multiple times to get it finally right.
I love the time I spent here with the PhD scholars and how much they helped me and basically treated me like a small kid.
I love the environment of a research lab!
